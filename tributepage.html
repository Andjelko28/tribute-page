Desmond Doss

A man who never carried a weapon.

Desmond Doss Biography

  • 1919 - Born in Lynchburg, Virginia.
  • 1919 - 1942 - He was raised as Seventh-day Adventist,not support violence and he was a vegeterian.
  • 1942 - 1945 - He became a combat-medic in USA Army in WW2,refuseing to carry a weapon or to kill someone. He saved 50-100 wounded infantryman during Okinawa battle and he was wounded and evacuated.
  • 1945 - 2006 - He had one child and was married two times. He died on March 23,2006 due to his bad health and he was burried in the National Cemetery in Chattanooga, Tennessee.
  • He was awarded with Medal of Honor for his actions in Okinawa.
